# photographyGuide.md
### Menashe Experience Language — Phase 6: Photography Language

---

Photography in Menashe Calendar must feel like it was captured by someone who
lives this heritage — not by a stock agency trying to represent it.

---

## Lighting

**Golden Hour is the default.**
All photography is shot or graded toward warm, directional late-afternoon or
early-morning light. Hard midday light is never used.

```
Target color temperature:  2800–4200 K (warm)
Highlights:                warm amber / gold
Shadows:                   deep burgundy / charcoal — never pure black
Midtones:                  warm cream
```

**Candlelight context (yahrzeit, Shabbat):**
Flames are the light source. Surrounding areas fall into warm shadow.
No artificial fill light visible.

**Night sky context (zmanim, havdalah):**
Stars are visible. Moon provides the primary light.
Color temperature: 3200 K (moonlight, slightly warm).
No blue-white cold light.

---

## Color Grading

All photos are graded before use. Unedited/ungraded photos are never shipped.

**Grade targets:**
- Lift shadows into deep amber-brown (never pure black)
- Pull highlights toward gold (#d4a843) rather than white
- Desaturate cool tones (blues, greens) by –20 to –35%
- Boost warm tones (reds, oranges, yellows) by +10 to +15%
- Add a subtle warm vignette at 20–30% opacity

**LUT / Grade name (internal):** "Jerusalem Warmth"

**Output color space:** sRGB. No wide-gamut output until iOS 16 minimum target is raised.

---

## Depth

**Shallow depth of field is preferred.**
The subject is sharp. The background is soft and warm.
`f/1.8 – f/2.8` equivalent for portrait subjects.
`f/5.6 – f/8` for landscape and architectural subjects.

**No flat, uniform-focus shots** unless the subject is an object on a plain surface
(Torah, kiddush cup, candles).

---

## Contrast

**Medium contrast.** Not high contrast (too harsh). Not low contrast (muddy).

- Clarity: +8 to +15 (mild midtone contrast)
- Dehaze: 0 (do not add artificial clarity)
- Exposure: 0 to +0.5 (slightly brighter than neutral)

---

## Crop

**Safe crop ratios:**
- Hero: 16:9 or 3:2 (landscape only)
- Feature card thumbnail: 4:3 or 1:1
- Gallery: 1:1 square
- Full-bleed background: 9:16 (portrait, fills screen)

**Focal point:** Always in the upper-left or center third. Never in the bottom third.
This ensures subject remains visible even when overlaid by card content.

**Never crop to show a person partially.** Either show them fully or not at all.

---

## Composition

**Rule of thirds.** Primary subject on a third intersection.

**Framing devices encouraged:**
- Stone archway framing the horizon
- Tree branches framing the sky
- A doorway framing a courtyard

**Horizon line:** Always level. Never tilted (dutch angle).

**Leading lines:** Paths, walls, and fences leading toward the horizon or subject.

**Clutter:** Zero. If a modern element (car, signage, power line) is in frame, either
shoot around it or remove it in post.

---

## Cultural Integrity

Every photograph used in Menashe Calendar must pass the following cultural review:

| Question | Required Answer |
|---|---|
| Does this photograph represent Benei Menashe or Israeli/Jewish contexts authentically? | Yes |
| Are any people depicted identifiable? | Only with written consent |
| Does the image contain religious objects treated respectfully? | Yes |
| Is any modern commercial branding visible? | No |
| Has a community representative reviewed images with people in them? | Yes |

**Generic stock photography is never used.** If authentic photography is unavailable,
gradient illustration (see illustrationGuide.md) is used as a placeholder until
proper photographs are commissioned.

---

## AI Image Generation Guidance

When using AI image generation (Midjourney, DALL·E, Stable Diffusion, Flux) to produce
photography-style backgrounds or illustrations:

**Approved prompt keywords:**
```
golden hour, Jerusalem limestone, ancient stone walls, olive grove, Judean hills,
warm morning light, candlelight, parchment texture, traditional Jewish architecture,
soft shadows, shallow depth of field, warm color grade, serene, respectful
```

**Forbidden prompt keywords:**
```
hyperrealistic faces, crowd scenes, stock photo, sharp contrast, cold lighting,
blue-tinted, nightclub, modern urban, generic travel photography
```

**Review required:** All AI-generated imagery must be reviewed by a human before
shipping. AI images are marked internally with a `[AI-generated]` tag in the asset
filename for audit purposes.

**Format:** WebP, 2× and 3× resolution, transparency supported where needed.

**Output dimensions (minimum):**
- Hero background: 828×552 @2× = 1656×1104px
- Feature card: 390×390 @2× = 780×780px
- Full-screen background: 390×844 @2× = 780×1688px
