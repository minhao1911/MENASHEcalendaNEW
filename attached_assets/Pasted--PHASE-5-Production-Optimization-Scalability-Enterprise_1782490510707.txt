# PHASE 5 — Production Optimization, Scalability & Enterprise Deployment

## OBJECTIVE

The Memorial Sanctuary is now visually complete.

Do NOT redesign the environment.

Do NOT rebuild the UI.

Do NOT regenerate assets.

This phase prepares the application for public release, ensuring enterprise-grade performance, scalability, security, and reliability while preserving the premium visual experience.

The application should feel as polished as Apple Maps, Airbnb, or Google Earth while supporting thousands of concurrent visitors.

---

# PERFORMANCE OPTIMIZATION

## Rendering

Optimize React Three Fiber rendering.

Implement:

• GPU Instancing for memorial candles

• Instanced vegetation

• Instanced flowers

• Instanced lanterns

• Hardware accelerated rendering

• Efficient shadow maps

• Batched rendering

Avoid unnecessary re-renders.

---

## Level of Detail (LOD)

Implement multiple quality levels.

Examples:

Trees

Buildings

Bridges

Flowers

Rocks

Candles

Distant objects should automatically switch to lower-detail models.

Nearby objects should display full quality.

---

## Texture Optimization

Convert textures to modern compressed formats.

Use:

KTX2

Basis Universal

WebP

AVIF

Generate mipmaps.

Target:

512px

1024px

2048px

4096px only where necessary.

---

## GLB Optimization

Compress every GLB model.

Use:

Draco Compression

Meshopt

KTX2 textures

Optimize:

Vertices

Draw calls

Mesh count

Animation data

---

## Lazy Loading

Load assets only when required.

Examples:

Nearby memorials

Nearby buildings

Waterfalls

Olive groves

Large texture sets

Load progressively as users explore.

---

## Frustum Culling

Render only visible objects.

Hidden candles should not consume GPU resources.

---

## Occlusion Culling

Hide objects blocked by terrain or architecture.

---

## Asset Streaming

Stream environment assets dynamically.

Avoid downloading the entire sanctuary at startup.
