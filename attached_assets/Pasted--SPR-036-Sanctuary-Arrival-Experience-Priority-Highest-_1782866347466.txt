# SPR-036 — Sanctuary Arrival Experience

## Priority

🟢 Highest Product Priority

This sprint defines the first emotional impression users experience when entering the Memorial Sanctuary.

No new features.

Only experience refinement.

---

# Mission

Transform the first 30 seconds inside SanctuaryWorld into a peaceful, intuitive, and emotionally meaningful arrival.

The Sanctuary should immediately communicate:

"You have entered a sacred place."

---

---

# Phase 1 — Landing Position

Spawn the visitor directly in front of the Memorial Gate.

Requirements:

✓ Face toward the valley.

✓ Memorial Gate centered.

✓ Welcome Board visible.

✓ Community Candle Board visible.

✓ Visitor Guide visible.

✓ Sacred Avenue directly ahead.

The visitor should never spawn facing a wall, mountain, or terrain.

---

# Phase 2 — Arrival Animation

Refine the entrance sequence.

Sequence:

Black Fade

↓

Fade to Sanctuary

↓

Camera settles naturally

↓

Ambient audio begins softly

↓

Player gains control

The transition should feel calm and respectful.

---

# Phase 3 — Entrance Boards

Verify the three entrance boards.

Welcome Board

- Welcome message
- Sanctuary purpose

Community Candle Board

- Recent candle offerings
- Memorial names
- Live community data

Visitor Guide

- Movement controls
- Reflection etiquette
- Sanctuary guidance

All boards must be readable from the spawn point.

---

# Phase 4 — Camera

Ensure the first camera view naturally frames:

✓ Memorial Gate

✓ Sacred Avenue

✓ Valley

✓ Tree of Remembrance (partially visible)

No abrupt rotations.

No disorientation.

---

# Phase 5 — Lighting

The entrance should receive:

- warm sunrise light
- gentle bloom
- visible volumetric rays
- soft shadow contrast

Avoid dark or flat lighting.

---

# Phase 6 — Atmosphere

Refine:

- fog density
- bloom intensity
- ambient color
- sky warmth

The Sanctuary should feel peaceful rather than dramatic.

---

# Phase 7 — Performance

Maintain:

Desktop

≈60 FPS

Average Android

40–60 FPS

Do not sacrifice smooth interaction for visual effects.
