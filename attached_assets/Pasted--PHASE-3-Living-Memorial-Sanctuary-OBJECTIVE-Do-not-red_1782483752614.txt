# PHASE 3 — Living Memorial Sanctuary

## OBJECTIVE

Do not redesign the environment created in Phase 2.

Instead, transform it into a living, breathing sanctuary.

The visitor should feel like they have entered a peaceful place of remembrance rather than a static 3D scene.

Maintain all existing functionality.

Preserve performance.

---

# CANDLE SYSTEM

Upgrade every memorial candle.

Each candle must have:

* realistic wax material
* physically inspired flame
* soft flickering animation
* warm point light
* subtle glow
* gentle smoke particles
* random flicker timing

When a new candle is lit:

* smooth placement animation
* soft golden pulse
* gentle camera focus
* warm sound effect
* nearby candles subtly react

Thousands of candles should be rendered efficiently using instancing.

---

# WATER

Bring the water to life.

Requirements:

* flowing rivers
* animated waterfalls
* moving surface ripples
* reflections
* shoreline animation
* mist around waterfalls
* soft water sounds

---

# TREES

Olive trees should gently move.

Requirements:

* wind animation
* subtle branch movement
* leaf motion
* randomized movement

Avoid exaggerated motion.

---

# FLOWERS AND GRASS

Animate vegetation.

Requirements:

* grass swaying
* flowers moving gently
* natural wind variation

---

# SKY

Create a living sky.

Include:

* moving clouds
* changing sunlight direction
* subtle atmospheric haze
* birds occasionally flying overhead

Do not use storm effects.

---

# LIGHTING

Enhance atmosphere.

Requirements:

* volumetric sunlight
* bloom around candles
* warm evening light
* realistic soft shadows
* subtle god rays through trees
* gentle fog

Everything should feel peaceful.

---

# MEMORIAL INTERACTION

Clicking a candle should:

* smoothly move the camera
* softly highlight the candle
* open the memorial profile
* dim the background slightly
* emphasize the selected memorial

Closing the profile should smoothly restore the scene.

---

# CAMERA

Improve movement.

Requirements:

* cinematic easing
* inertia
* smooth pan
* smooth zoom
* no sudden jumps

Camera should feel premium.

---

# AMBIENT LIFE

Add subtle environmental life.

Include:

* birds
* butterflies
* drifting leaves
* floating pollen
* soft dust particles

Do not add human characters or animals that distract from remembrance.

---

# AUDIO

Create a peaceful soundscape.

Include:

* flowing water
* birdsong
* gentle wind
* soft piano
* light orchestral ambience

Music should never overpower the environment.

---

# MEMORIAL PROFILE

Upgrade the experience.

Profile should animate smoothly.

Include:

* premium glass panel
* soft background blur
* elegant transitions
* smooth fade in/out
* high-quality typography
* candle count
* flower count
* visitor count
* remembrance message

---

# MICRO ANIMATIONS

Every interaction should feel polished.

Examples:

* button hover animations
* soft icon animations
* candle glow pulses
* panel transitions
* subtle UI motion

Avoid flashy effects.

---

# PERFORMANCE

Maintain smooth rendering.

Requirements:

* GPU instancing
* Level of Detail
* Frustum culling
* Lazy asset loading
* Efficient particle systems

Targets:

Desktop: 60 FPS

Modern Mobile: 45–60 FPS

---

# DO NOT

Do not redesign the layout.

Do not replace the environment again.

Do not add fantasy elements.

Do not create a game.

Do not add combat, scores, or unnecessary effects.

The focus must remain on peace, remembrance, and spirituality.

---

# SUCCESS CRITERIA

By the end of Phase 3:

* The sanctuary feels alive.
* Candles flicker naturally.
* Rivers and waterfalls flow continuously.
* Trees and vegetation move gently.
* The sky evolves subtly.
* Camera movement feels cinematic.
* Every interaction feels calm and respectful.
* Visitors experience an emotional, immersive place of remembrance rather than a typical website.
