# SPR-037A — Sanctuary Grounded Movement Upgrade

## Priority

🟢 Version 1.0 Polish

NO new features.

NO database changes.

NO API changes.

NO new UI.

Improve only the feeling of walking inside SanctuaryWorld.

---

# Mission

The Sanctuary currently feels like:

- flying
- floating
- riding a merry-go-round
- gliding over the terrain

It should instead feel like:

- walking on sacred ground
- having body weight
- moving naturally
- being physically present inside the valley

The goal is to make SanctuaryWorld feel closer to a premium AAA exploration experience.

---

# Phase 1 — Camera Height

The camera currently feels too high.

Lower the first-person eye level to realistic human height.

Target:

• Eye height ≈ 1.65–1.75 m

The visitor should feel like standing on the pathway, not hovering above it.

---

# Phase 2 — Field of View

Current FOV still feels too wide.

Evaluate a cinematic FOV between:

60°–68°

Choose the value that feels most natural while walking.

Avoid fisheye distortion.

---

# Phase 3 — Walking Physics

Walking should have weight.

Tune movement:

• Lower walk speed slightly
• Reduce acceleration
• Reduce deceleration
• Add smooth easing
• Remove skating/gliding feeling

Movement should resemble a respectful walking pace.

---

# Phase 4 — Camera Motion

Implement subtle natural body movement.

When walking:

✓ gentle head bob
✓ tiny vertical movement
✓ slight left/right sway
✓ subtle breathing motion

Requirements:

• extremely subtle
• no nausea
• automatically disabled when prefers-reduced-motion is enabled

---

# Phase 5 — Rotation

Current turning feels too loose.

Improve:

• smoother mouse look
• gentle damping
• lower rotation sensitivity
• remove spinning sensation

The visitor should feel connected to the environment.

---

# Phase 6 — Ground Presence

Strengthen the feeling of being on the ground.

Verify:

✓ feet feel close to terrain
✓ no hovering above paths
✓ camera follows terrain correctly
✓ no clipping through slopes
✓ no sudden height jumps

---

# Phase 7 — Collision

Audit every walkable area.

Fix:

• invisible walls
• snagging on rocks
• walking through objects
• terrain clipping
• floating over stairs

The player should naturally follow paths.

---

# Phase 8 — Environmental Feedback

Increase the sense of presence using existing systems.

Improve:

• candle glow nearby
• tree shadows
• ground texture detail
• stone path readability
• fog depth
• atmospheric perspective

Do NOT add new assets.

Use existing scene elements more effectively.

---

# Phase 9 — Audio Presence

Refine existing ambient sound.

Walking should feel alive through:

• gentle wind
• distant water
• soft birds
• subtle candle ambience

No loud or distracting effects.

---

# Phase 10 — Camera Focus

As visitors approach:

• Memorial Gate
• Tree of Remembrance
• Reflection Pool
• Prayer Pavilion

Use subtle camera framing only.

Do NOT take control away from the user.

No forced cinematic sequences.

---

# Performance

Maintain:

Desktop:
≈60 FPS

Average Android:
40–60 FPS

Do not introduce heavy post-processing or expensive shaders.

---

# Constraints

DO NOT:

❌ Add new features

❌ Add new UI

❌ Add new navigation

❌ Add new databases

❌ Add new APIs

❌ Add new scene objects

❌ Change business logic

Focus entirely on movement, camera, immersion, and realism.

---

# Deliverables

Provide:

1. Camera tuning report
2. Movement tuning report
3. Collision audit
4. Grounding improvements
5. Performance verification
6. Before/after comparison of movement feel

---

# Success Criteria

A first-time visitor should immediately feel:

• "I am walking through a real memorial sanctuary."

They should never feel:

• like they are flying
• like they are floating
• like they are riding a merry-go-round
• like they are controlling a game camera

The Sanctuary should feel calm, grounded, reverent, and physically believable.

Stop after verification.
