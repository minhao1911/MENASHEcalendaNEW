# PHASE 2 — AAA Environment Reconstruction

## OBJECTIVE

Do NOT redesign the application.

Do NOT modify the memorial system.

Do NOT change routing, authentication, Supabase integration, or UI logic.

This phase is ONLY about rebuilding the 3D environment to production quality.

Use the uploaded reference image as the primary artistic direction.

The goal is to transform the current low-poly prototype into a realistic, cinematic Community Memorial Sanctuary.

---

# TERRAIN

Delete the current flat terrain.

Create a sculpted memorial valley with natural elevation.

Include:

* rolling hills
* gentle slopes
* cliffs
* terraces
* natural depressions
* stone retaining walls
* landscaped memorial gardens

Terrain should feel handcrafted instead of procedural.

---

# ENVIRONMENT LAYOUT

Rebuild the valley similar to the uploaded reference.

Include:

• Central memorial sanctuary

• Multiple terraces

• Stone bridges

• Reflection pools

• Flowing rivers

• Waterfalls

• Olive groves

• Prayer gardens

• Candle gathering areas

• Scenic viewpoints

Everything should guide visitors naturally through the sanctuary.

---

# ARCHITECTURE

Replace placeholder buildings.

Use realistic Jerusalem limestone architecture.

Include:

* limestone walls
* arches
* columns
* stairways
* prayer terraces
* memorial courtyards
* stone pavilions
* elegant gateways

Avoid castles or fantasy structures.

The architecture should feel timeless and sacred.

---

# OLIVE TREES

Delete every low-poly tree.

Replace them with realistic mature olive trees.

Requirements:

* twisted trunks
* dense foliage
* natural size variation
* random rotation
* subtle wind animation

Scatter trees naturally throughout the valley.

---

# VEGETATION

Add Mediterranean landscaping.

Include:

* lavender
* white flowers
* grass variation
* shrubs
* cypress trees
* native plants

Avoid repetitive placement.

---

# WATER SYSTEM

Replace all existing water.

Create:

* flowing rivers
* realistic waterfalls
* reflection pools
* animated water shaders
* soft ripples
* natural riverbanks

Use reflective water materials with realistic lighting.

---

# PATHWAYS

Replace existing paths.

Use:

Jerusalem limestone paving

Natural stone stairs

Garden walkways

Small bridges

Curved paths

Everything should encourage exploration.

---

# MEMORIAL ZONES

Create designated memorial candle areas.

Each zone should contain:

stone flooring

garden edges

olive trees

flower beds

warm lighting

The environment should feel peaceful rather than crowded.

---

# MATERIAL QUALITY

Do not use flat colors.

Every major surface must use high-quality PBR materials.

Examples:

Grass

Stone

Limestone

Wood

Marble

Soil

Rock

Water

Each material should support:

* Albedo
* Normal
* Roughness
* Ambient Occlusion

---

# LIGHTING

Continue using the Phase 1 rendering pipeline.

Adjust lighting to resemble:

Golden hour in Jerusalem.

Requirements:

Warm sunlight

Soft shadows

Volumetric light

Natural ambient lighting

No harsh white lighting.

---

# ATMOSPHERE

Add:

soft fog

dust particles

floating pollen

light breeze

gentle cloud movement

Everything should enhance serenity.

---

# PERFORMANCE

Maintain excellent performance.

Use:

* Instanced vegetation
* Level of Detail (LOD)
* Frustum culling
* Lazy loading
* Compressed textures
* Optimized GLB models

Target:

Desktop: 60 FPS

Modern Mobile: 45–60 FPS

---

# DO NOT

Do not add placeholder cubes.

Do not create low-poly trees.

Do not use primitive cylinders for architecture.

Do not regenerate existing memorial functionality.

Do not redesign the UI.

---

# SUCCESS CRITERIA

By the end of Phase 2:

* The valley closely resembles the uploaded reference image.
* The environment feels handcrafted and premium.
* Visitors immediately experience a peaceful, sacred atmosphere.
* The sanctuary no longer looks like a prototype and is ready for Phase 3 (interactive effects, candle animations, advanced atmosphere, and world polish).
