Recent architecture sprints (SPR-034 through SPR-035) removed duplicate Memorial implementations and simplified navigation.

However, the current runtime behaviour indicates the production SanctuaryWorld may no longer be the implementation being rendered.

Observed symptoms include:

- Entrance Gate not visible.
- Welcome Board missing.
- Community Candle Board missing.
- Visitor Guide missing.
- Camera still spawning in the old location.
- Camera facing terrain instead of the Sanctuary.
- Spawn orientation unchanged.
- Runtime behaviour does not match previous implementation reports.

This audit must determine the exact production runtime path.

No assumptions.

Evidence only.
